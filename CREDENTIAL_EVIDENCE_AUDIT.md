# Digital Credential Evidence Audit

## Verification Criteria for Digital Credentials

A credential is considered DIGITAL only if it has:
- ✅ Mobile app OR
- ✅ Digital wallet integration OR
- ✅ Online/remote verification capability OR
- ✅ QR code / NFC / cryptographic verification OR
- ✅ Official documentation stating "digital ID"
